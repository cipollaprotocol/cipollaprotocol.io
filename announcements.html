Mint price reduced to 0.005 ETH

The mint price has been reduced from 0.05 ETH to 0.005 ETH, effective July 14, 2026.

Cipolla Protocol V1 is being used as a technical sandbox to test the Provenance Royalties mechanism in real on-chain conditions. Tokens will be minted across multiple addresses to generate real distribution data and test all protocol functions — including P2P sales, marketplace listings, and royalty claims.

This activity is documented here to be fully transparent and avoid any confusion with artificial volume inflation.
