Founding Nodes.

The 249 addresses that mint directly from the Layer Zero contract are the protocol's Founding Nodes — permanently, regardless of whether they later sell their token.

Founding Node status is tied to the act of minting, not to holding. It cannot be transferred, lost, or revoked. Like the Provenance Royalties mechanism itself, early participation creates permanent rights.

As a Founding Node :

→ Access to a private Discord channel with the Cipolla Protocol team on future development directions

→ Priority mint window for future collections — before public opening

→ Option to participate in any future governance mechanism — at your discretion, no obligation

Public intentions, not contractual commitments. No financial returns guaranteed.

Royalty simulator
Enter a resale price and number of historical owners to see how royalties distribute. This is a simulation — see note below.

Note on this simulation. For P2P sales via buyP2P(): the transaction is atomic — payment, token transfer, and royalty distribution happen in a single on-chain operation. Either everything executes or nothing does. Royalties are calculated directly from msg.value, making the distribution exact and trustless.

For marketplace sales: many marketplaces apply zero-royalty policies, undermining the value chain Provenance Royalties is designed to protect. Marketplace buyers are NOT added to the ownership history and receive no future royalties. Existing holders still receive their share via distributeRoyalties() — called manually by the contract owner (restricted after an external audit to prevent wash trading).

Use this to understand the mechanism, not as a precise financial calculator.
